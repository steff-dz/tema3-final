Custom Orders

To order a custom piece of furniture, we have provided a form that will take your contact information, details on the piece you would like to order, and your budget.

Before you proceed to the order form, please consider that custom work is costly. Besides paying for hand craftmanship, the price must also cover the cost of all materials. Certain materials are more expensive than others, which will factor into your quote.

If you have any questions along the way, there is space to include them at the end of the form.
